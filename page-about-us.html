ABOUT HAIRGROW23

Hairgro23 is the range of Natural Haircare products for Hair Regrowth, Dandruff Control, Regulating Sebum production & making hair Strong & Healthy.

The products treat hair loss effectively by stimulating new hair follicles & strengthening the hair from roots to strands, thus reducing hair fall. They also nourish the scalp with necessary vitamins & minerals.

There are about 46 power ingredients that stimulate new hair growth & strengthen the hair. Different combinations are carefully made in each product, to deliver the best results.
